Data sources
- CLS_rv_raw: Rosenthal et al. (2021) -- contains data from Keck/HIRES (pre and post 2004), APF/Levy, and Lick/Hamilton.
	https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021ApJS..255....8R/abstract 
- HJS_rv_raw: Wittenmyer et al. (2007) -- contains data from McDonald Observatory 2.7-m Harlan J. Smith Telescope.
	https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2007ApJ...654..625W/abstract
- CORALIE_rv_raw: Naef et al. (2001) -- contains data from the CORALIE echelle spectrograph mounted on the 1.2-m ``Leonard Euler'' Swiss Telescope.
	https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2001A%26A...375..205N/abstract
